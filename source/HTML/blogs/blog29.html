• Cosine similarity can be used to calculate text similarity, the specific steps are as follows:

    1: Calculate the frequency of each word in both texts.

    2: Get the word frequency matrix from the word frequency.

    Example: A:[1,2,4,2,1,3]   B:[2,3,4,1,2,3].

    3: The similarity is obtained by the law of cosines.

    4: The result is the similarity between the two sentences.
